Charity On Top

Runner-up for B~STEM WeHackToo Hackathon

Many individuals within every community are often passionate about a certain cause, but many fail to act upon that passion due to a lack of motivation. My team and I found this especially troubling when we realized the multitude of simple ways that people could contribute. Of those consisted of local businesses that partner up with charities to create "charity deals", or a product or service, that upon purchase, will allow the business to donate a certain portion of proceeds to their partnering charity. Charity deals often allow the individual to support a cause without directly spending extra money or time to do so.

Collaboration with Katia Villevald and Hannah Yan
